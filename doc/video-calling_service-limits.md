# Service limits

Video Calling usage is subject to service limits. Regularly monitor the Peak Concurrent Users (PCU) and bandwidth usage of your project to ensure these remain within the quota benchmarks, preventing disruptions to service. If your business growth requires higher quotas, contact Agora [technical support](https://docs-md.agora.io/en/mailto:support@agora.io.md).

## Quota benchmarks by region

For basic real-time interaction use-cases, the quota benchmark is considered reached when either the PCU or bandwidth usage meets the specified limits.

| Item      | America | Europe  | Asia    | China   | 
|:----------|:--------|:--------|:--------|:--------| 
| PCU       | 10,000  | 10,000  | 10,000  | 10,000  | 
| Bandwidth | 10 Gbps | 10 Gbps | 10 Gbps | 10 Gbps |
